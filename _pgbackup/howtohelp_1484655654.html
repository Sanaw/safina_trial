Volunteer

We have many opportunities for volunteers. If you’re interested or talented in a specific area, we most likely can use you!

Donate

You can make a difference today, give what you can to those that need it most – every bit helps! All funds donated to Safina Children home will be used in one of our many project locations to help only the poorest of the poor. None of your contributions will ever go to administrative costs or other expenses.

Sponsor a Child

Our Orphan programs are sponsorship funded projects that provide children from impoverished backgrounds with no means to go to school a chance for an education and gives orphaned children from the slums and tribal areas a safe home.
